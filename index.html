"Blind Dating That Helps The World See"

100% of proceeds are donated to OneSight, a nonprofit organization bringing vision care access to those who need it most.

This fall, we are offering three separate registration periods, where all matches will be released during the last week of the period. Each registration period will also have their own unique giveaways, so stay tuned! They will take place in the following weeks:

August 23 through September 12
September 13 through October 17
October 17 through November 21

Seeing Clearly Changes Lives

"Right now, 1 in every 7 people around the world are faced with a challenge that holds them back from employment, education, and social interaction. Well, we believe a brighter future starts with clear sight. That’s why OneSight is dedicated to getting those people one thing that can change their future now and for good – a simple pair of glasses. As simple as glasses are, more than a billion people in the world need them but don’t have access to get them. We call this the vision gap, and closing it isn’t simple – but OneSight has the solution and we need your help. With clear sight, we can change lives, communities, and hearts for good, paving the way toward a brighter future. Be a changemaker and help us change the future for good."

— OneSight

100% of our proceeds go to OneSight!
