Why become a matchmaker with Dating Deacons?

In short, we are an inclusive, energetic, easy, and entertaining group to work with. We have a blast making pairings and helping connect WFU one blind date at a time.

In long... When we have enough registrations to make a round of pairings, all the matchmakers will meet on a Sunday afternoon to prepare matches. These pairing meetings typically take around 2 hours and occur 3-4 times a semester. As a matchmaker you will get to pair your friends and other wake students with someone that you think they would really enjoy and it is an exhilarating feeling to say the least. What if something comes of the date? Will your friend finally meet someone they truly click with? Long term, what if you are the one that introduced them and they start dating? Only you will have the inside information to make this happen! Any group on campus will tell you that their club is an easy addition to a resume. Ours might just be the best, and here's why. Imagine being able to tell potential employers that you were a part of the executive board to the largest blind dating club on campus. Oh, and on top of that, they support a charity? Even cooler. Questions will ensue about why and what sort of work you did. This is the type of material that will separate your resume from the ones before it.

What are we looking for?

We are looking for matchmakers that will allow us to reach a larger percentage of the student population. We are looking for people that will question our current methods and suggest alternative ways of the pairing, encountering, or matchmaking processes. Most of all, we want matchmakers that will help us promote Dating Deacons.
