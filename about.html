Why Dating Deacons?

"Over the summer I found that my own parents met on a blind date. I was dissapointed by the lack of opportunities to do this on Wake's campus, and with help from a small group of friends, we were able to make an algorithm that would allow similar people from different groups on campus to meet each other. As for the cause that the proceeds support, I am essentially blind without glasses and wanted to to help those less fortunate get the benefit of clear vision as well."

TED MIDDLETON

FOUNDER & PRESIDENT
